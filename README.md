# Super-Sales-Dashboard ( Interactive dashoard using Excel)
## Project Objective
A clean and interactive dashboard showcasing sales, profit, and customer insights. It highlights performance by segment, payment mode, shipping type, and product categories, with clear visuals to track trends and business growth.

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset used in this project can be accessed here:  
[SuperStore Sales DataSet.xlsx](https://github.com/Shruti19-17/Super-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/SuperStore%20Sales%20DataSet.xlsx.xlsx)

## Essential  Question (KPIs) :
1.Total Sales Revenue – Overall sales generated (e.g., 6.42K).

2.Profit Margin – Net profit compared to sales, showing profitability.

3.Sales by Customer Segment – Consumer, Corporate, Home Office distribution.

4.Sales by Product Category – Office Supplies, Furniture, Technology.

5.Top Sub-Category Sales – Binders, Furnishings, Art.

6.Sales by Payment Mode – Online, COD, Cards.

7.Sales by Shipping Mode – Standard, First Class, Same Day, Second Class.

8.Regional Sales Performance – Central, East, South, West.

9.Monthly/Quarterly Sales Trends – Tracking growth and seasonal spikes.

10.Quantity Sold vs. Profit – To identify high-volume but low-profit items.

## Process
1.Data Collection – Gathered the SuperStore Sales dataset.

2.Data Preparation – Cleaned, formatted, and standardized data.

3.Data Modeling – Structured dataset into dimensions (time, region, category, segment).

4.Dashboard Design – Defined KPIs and created interactive visuals.

5.Visualization & Insights – Highlighted top-performing categories, customer segments, and trends.

6.Deployment – Published dashboard and dataset link on GitHub with documentation.

## Project Insights (Few Lines)
1,Sales volume is strong (6.42K) but overall profit is negative (-161.02), showing a need for better cost control.

2.Consumer segment (52%) drives most revenue, while Office Supplies are the top-performing category.

3.Online payments (49%) dominate, and Standard shipping (4.4K) is the most preferred mode.

4.Seasonal spikes in March and December highlight opportunities for targeted campaigns.

## Conclusion
The Super Store Sales Dashboard provides a clear view of business performance by combining sales, profit, customer, and operational insights. While sales volume is strong, the negative profit highlights the need for better pricing and cost management. Consumer segments and office supplies drive the majority of revenue, with online payments and standard shipping being the most preferred choices. Seasonal spikes in demand present opportunities for targeted campaigns. Overall, the dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions
