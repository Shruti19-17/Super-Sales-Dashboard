# Super-Sales-Dashboard ( Interactive dashoard using Excel)
## Project Objective
A clean and interactive dashboard showcasing sales, profit, and customer insights. It highlights performance by segment, payment mode, shipping type, and product categories, with clear visuals to track trends and business growth.

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset used in this project can be accessed here:  
[SuperStore Sales DataSet.xlsx](https://github.com/Shruti19-17/Super-Sales-Dashboard/blob/main/SuperStore%20Sales%20DataSet.xlsx.xlsx)

🎯 Essential KPIs
Total Sales Revenue – Overall sales generated (e.g., 6.42K).

Profit Margin – Net profit compared to sales, showing profitability.

Sales by Customer Segment – Consumer, Corporate, Home Office distribution.

Sales by Product Category – Office Supplies, Furniture, Technology.

Top Sub-Category Sales – Binders, Furnishings, Art.

Sales by Payment Mode – Online, COD, Cards.

Sales by Shipping Mode – Standard, First Class, Same Day, Second Class.

Regional Sales Performance – Central, East, South, West.

Monthly/Quarterly Sales Trends – Tracking growth and seasonal spikes.

Quantity Sold vs. Profit – To identify high-volume but low-profit items.
